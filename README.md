# 💧 Hydro-Resource-Hub

A curated collection of open-source software, public datasets, and computational tools for Water Resources Engineering and hydrological modelling.

## 🗺️ GIS & Spatial Analysis
* [QGIS](https://qgis.org/) - A free and open-source geographic information system for mapping and spatial data analysis.
* [GRASS GIS](https://grass.osgeo.org/) - Used for geospatial data management, image processing, and spatial modelling.

## 🌊 Hydrological & Hydraulic Modeling
* [EPA SWMM](https://www.epa.gov/water-research/storm-water-management-model-swmm) - Storm Water Management Model for urban runoff simulation.
* [EPANET](https://www.epa.gov/water-research/epanet) - Software that models the hydraulic and water quality behaviour of water distribution piping systems.
* [HEC-RAS](https://www.hec.usace.army.mil/software/hec-ras/) - River Analysis System for modelling water flowing through systems of open channels.

## 📊 Public Datasets
* [USGS Water Data](https://waterdata.usgs.gov/nwis) - Global and US-based water resources data.
* [India WRIS](https://indiawris.gov.in/wris/) - Water Resources Information System for regional hydrological data.
* [EarthExplorer](https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/) - Access to satellite imagery and digital elevation models (DEMs).

## 🐍 Python Libraries for Water Engineers
* `geopandas` - Simplifies working with geospatial data in Python.
* `rasterio` - Reads and writes geospatial raster data (like satellite images).
* `HydroStats` - Tools for calculating error metrics for hydrological models.
